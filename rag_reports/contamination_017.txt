**Quality Control Report**

**Defect Summary**

A foreign object is sticking out of a clear glass bottle, classified as a contamination defect. The anomaly score is 0.0904.

**Probable Causes**

1. Improper cleaning or inspection process, allowing debris to enter the bottle.
2. Human error during packaging or handling, resulting in an object being inserted into the bottle.
3. Contamination during manufacturing or storage, such as dust or fibers being introduced into the bottle.

**Corrective Actions**

1. Review and improve cleaning and inspection procedures to prevent debris entry.
2. Implement additional quality control measures during packaging and handling to prevent human error.
3. Conduct a thorough investigation into the manufacturing and storage processes to identify and address any potential contamination sources.

**Urgency Level: Medium**

The defect is considered medium-urgency due to the presence of a foreign object in the bottle, which could potentially affect the product's performance or customer safety. However, the object's size and shape do not pose an immediate risk, allowing for a medium-urgency classification.
